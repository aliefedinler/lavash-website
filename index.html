The story

Made the way it should be.

Lavash is Paterson's home for Turkish street food done right. We're built around one thing: the dürüm — a hand-rolled lavash wrap stuffed with charcoal-grilled kebab, fresh vegetables, and house-made sauces.

Our specialty is Adana — hand-minced, hand-skewered, grilled over open flame the way it's done in southern Turkey. Wrap it in warm tandır bread and you've got the kind of meal people travel for.

Quick lunch, family dinner, or late-night craving — there's a dürüm here made the way it should be.
